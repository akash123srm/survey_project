Information Need:

An information need describes the information that needs to be satisfied. It is the information which user demands to satisfy his/her specific need.

Time Constraint:

Time is a really important aspect for an information need and its satisfaction. So this first axes signifies if the user addresses his information need to at a specific time or not.

Validity of Answer:

It signifies dependence between the time and the information need and the answer, which satisfies the information need.i.e. How long the answer to a corresponding information need will be valid.

Generality of Applicability:

It signifies whether the information need is for a particular user or if other users could have the same information need.

Location Constraint:

It signifies,if an information need is influenced by the location i.e. if location is an important factor in providing an answer to the user's information need.

Knowledge Codification:

Knowledge codification denotes how codified the knowledge that is needed to satisfy the information need is .i.e. if the knowledge needed to satisfy user's information need is a commonly accepted knowledge or fact for e.g.in form of books or articles or if it is something very widely discussed or controversial.
